# BatiConnect - One-Page Memo

## In one line

BatiConnect helps multi-building property managers in `VD/GE` know, before works start, what is proven, what is missing, and whether a pollutant dossier is actually ready for the authority.

## What problem it solves

Pre-work pollutant dossiers are fragmented across emails, drives, ERP attachments, lab annexes, and PDF reports. They often look documented without being procedurally ready. That creates authority returns, documentary rework, delayed starts, and weak proof of diligence.

## What it is

BatiConnect is a `readiness layer + proof engine` for regulated pre-work pollutant dossiers.

It is not:

- a property ERP
- a diagnostic firm
- a legal certification tool
- an AI chatbot for buildings

## Who buys first

The first buyer is the technical lead inside a multi-building property manager. This buyer feels the pain directly, coordinates the workflow, and can justify budget on avoided rework and readiness clarity.

## Why the wedge is credible

The wedge is narrow enough to sell:

- `VD/GE`
- pollutant dossiers
- before works
- property managers

It is also deep enough to compound:

- canton rule packs
- evidence chain with provenance
- living building memory
- correction flywheel from real dossiers

## Why this can become important

If BatiConnect wins the first wedge, it can accumulate assets that get stronger with usage:

- regulatory depth by canton
- reusable proof tied to a building over time
- traceable building memory
- faster, more precise dossier evaluation from real corrections

The moat does not come from AI alone. It comes from regulatory depth, memory, and trust.
