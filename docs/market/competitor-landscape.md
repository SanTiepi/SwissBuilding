## Strategic reading

### What matters now

SwissBuildingOS does not mainly compete against one obvious software leader in the wedge.
It competes against:
- fragmented expert workflows
- cantonal and legal complexity
- incumbent-system inertia
- weak documentation continuity

### What matters later

Longer term, the more serious threats come from:
- construction/documentation tools moving toward stronger proof workflows
- portfolio/decarbonization tools moving downward into building-level evidence
- incumbent property systems extending into workflow intelligence

### Positioning implication

SwissBuildingOS should present itself as:

**the Building Evidence Layer that connects regulated pre-work proof, living building memory, and later portfolio decisions**

not as:
- a chantier task tool
- a general document platform
- a property ERP
- a generic ESG planner
