# Jurisdiction Language and Terminology Pack

Date de controle: `25 mars 2026`

## Purpose

SwissBuilding operates across jurisdictions and languages.

The product should stay consistent even when terms differ between:

- federal
- cantonal
- communal
- French
- German
- Italian

## Rule

Terminology should be mapped, not flattened blindly.

## Focus

- authority naming
- procedure naming
- dossier and proof terminology
- user-facing clarity with jurisdiction-aware wording

## Acceptance

This pack is succeeding when the product remains understandable and credible
across Swiss language and jurisdiction boundaries.
