# Partner Certification and Quality Loop Pack

Date de controle: `25 mars 2026`

## Purpose

If SwissBuilding wants ecosystem depth, it should eventually distinguish
partners who operate well within the workflow from those who create rework.

## Rule

Certification should emerge from product-truth and workflow quality, not from
marketing labels.

## Inputs

- proof delivery quality
- response discipline
- rework rate
- trust signals
- handoff quality

## Acceptance

This pack is succeeding when partner quality can be discussed with evidence and
improved through the product loop.
