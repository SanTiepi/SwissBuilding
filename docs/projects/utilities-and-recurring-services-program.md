# Utilities and Recurring Services Program

## Mission

Make recurring building services first-class:
- utilities
- meters
- service vendors
- recurring invoices
- maintenance SLA and renewal signals

## Why This Matters

A building lifecycle OS cannot stop at one-off projects.
It must understand recurring operational costs, contracts, and service dependencies.

## Core Outcomes

1. utility/service contracts are modeled explicitly.
2. recurring service obligations can trigger signals and actions.
3. building systems and live performance can connect to recurring cost and vendor truth.

## Candidate Objects

- `UtilityAccount`
- `MeterContract`
- `ServiceVendorContract`
- `RecurringInvoiceMemory`
- `ServiceRenewalSignal`

## Acceptance

- recurring services are linked to the building and systems model
- cost and service memory becomes reusable across operations and finance layers
