# Incident, Emergency, and Continuity Program

## Mission

Add a first-class incident and continuity layer:
- incident record
- emergency readiness
- building shutdown state
- crisis communication
- incident-to-claim bridge

## Why This Matters

The building truth layer must also work under stress:
- damage
- shutdown
- urgent safety events
- insurer notifications
- continuity decisions

## Core Outcomes

1. incidents become durable building memory.
2. emergency states can temporarily override normal readiness.
3. claims and insurer-facing surfaces can start from structured incident truth.

## Candidate Objects

- `IncidentRecord`
- `EmergencyReadiness`
- `BuildingShutdownState`
- `CrisisCommunicationPack`
- `IncidentClaimBridge`

## Acceptance

- emergency and claims flows are represented without bypassing proof and auditability
- the product gains a continuity layer instead of assuming calm conditions only
