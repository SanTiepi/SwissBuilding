# Circularity and Material Afterlife Program

## Mission

Extend post-works truth into material afterlife:
- removed material passport
- disposal chain expansion
- reuse/salvage candidate tracking
- circularity score
- waste trace packs

## Why This Matters

The system should not stop at "removed".
It should know:
- how materials exited
- whether they were reused or destroyed
- what regulated afterlife they entered

## Core Outcomes

1. removed materials gain an afterlife record.
2. disposal and reuse become part of the evidence chain.
3. circularity becomes measurable, not rhetorical.

## Candidate Objects

- `RemovedMaterialPassport`
- `MaterialAfterlifeRecord`
- `ReuseCandidate`
- `CircularityScore`
- `WasteTracePack`

## Acceptance

- post-works truth expands into material traceability
- waste and circularity become portfolio and compliance signals
