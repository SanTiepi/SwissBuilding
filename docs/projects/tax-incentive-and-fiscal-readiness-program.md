# Tax, Incentive, and Fiscal Readiness Program

## Mission

Add a fiscal/public-incentive layer:
- tax-document pack
- grant timeline
- incentive eligibility
- rebate memory
- incentive loss risk

## Why This Matters

Many building decisions are blocked or accelerated by:
- subsidies
- tax timing
- incentive eligibility
- missing fiscal evidence

## Core Outcomes

1. funding and fiscal readiness become explicit product states.
2. building packs can serve both technical and fiscal workflows.
3. the portfolio layer can reason about timing against incentives.

## Candidate Objects

- `TaxIncentiveCase`
- `GrantTimeline`
- `RebateEligibility`
- `FiscalReadiness`
- `IncentiveLossRisk`

## Acceptance

- incentive and tax readiness are tied to real dossier truth
- the product can support funding timing without drifting into generic accounting
