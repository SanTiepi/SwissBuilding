# Trust Claims and Evidence Matrix

Date de controle: `25 mars 2026`

## Purpose

SwissBuilding will make many implicit claims.

This pack makes those claims explicit and ties each one to product evidence.

## Rule

If the product claims something important, it should be able to show the
supporting evidence path.

## Claim 1 - "This building is understood"

Evidence path:

- timeline continuity
- current blockers
- obligations
- diagnostic and proof assets

## Claim 2 - "This dossier is procedurally ready"

Evidence path:

- permit need clarity
- procedure step state
- no hidden complement or blocker
- proof requirement visibility

## Claim 3 - "This proof is reusable"

Evidence path:

- source
- version
- freshness
- caveats
- delivery or acknowledgement trace

## Claim 4 - "This authority route is credible"

Evidence path:

- rule applicability
- authority mapping
- local review semantics
- confidence and review state

## Claim 5 - "This pack is audience-appropriate"

Evidence path:

- inclusion and exclusion semantics
- audience preset
- proof freshness
- delivery trace

## Acceptance

This matrix is succeeding when important product claims are grounded in visible
proof instead of persuasion.
