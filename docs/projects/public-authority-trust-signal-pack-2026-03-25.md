# Public Authority Trust Signal Pack

Date de controle: `25 mars 2026`

## Purpose

If SwissBuilding wants to become easier for authorities and public actors to
work with, it must emit trustworthy signals, not just larger dossiers.

## Trust signals

### Signal 1 - route clarity

The product can show:

- which authority is expected
- why
- what confidence level applies

### Signal 2 - proof completeness

The product can show:

- what is attached
- what is missing
- what is stale

### Signal 3 - response traceability

The product can show:

- what was requested
- what was sent back
- what was acknowledged

### Signal 4 - local variability honesty

The product can show:

- when commune or canton specifics are manual-review dependent

### Signal 5 - version discipline

The product can show:

- which version was active at send time
- what later superseded it

## Acceptance

This pack is succeeding when SwissBuilding looks easier to trust because it is
clearer, not because it looks more formal.
