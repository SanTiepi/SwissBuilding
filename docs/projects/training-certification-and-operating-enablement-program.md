# Training, Certification, and Operating Enablement Program

## Mission

Add an enablement layer for operators, partners, and QA reviewers:
- operating readiness
- contractor competency memory
- mandatory training packs
- SOP library
- reviewer certification surfaces

## Why This Matters

As SwissBuilding becomes a larger operating layer, consistent execution depends on:
- people knowing what to do
- roles being competent
- reviews being standardized

## Core Outcomes

1. training and certification become linked to execution quality.
2. SOPs become reusable operating artifacts.
3. partner and internal quality can be strengthened without ad hoc side docs.

## Candidate Objects

- `OperatorReadiness`
- `TrainingPack`
- `SOPPlaybook`
- `ReviewerCertification`
- `CompetencyMemory`

## Acceptance

- enablement is modeled as part of operating quality, not as generic LMS scope
- the layer compounds quality, trust, and partner reputation
